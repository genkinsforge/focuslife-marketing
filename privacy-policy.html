1. Introduction

Welcome to FocusLife.ai, an AI-powered wellness tracking and insights platform operated by Genkins Forge LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal and health information.

Important: FocusLife.ai is designed for wellness tracking and is NOT a medical device or healthcare service. Our platform does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for medical decisions.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use our service. By using FocusLife.ai, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Our Privacy Principles

  • Data Minimization: We only collect information necessary to provide and improve our services
  • User Control: You control your data sharing preferences and can export or delete your information
  • Transparency: We clearly explain what data we collect and how we use it
  • Security First: We implement enterprise-grade security measures to protect your information
  • No Surprise Sharing: We do not sell your personal health data to third parties

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement enterprise-grade security measures to protect your information:

Security Layer Implementation Purpose
Encryption TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest Protect data during transmission and storage
Authentication JWT tokens, bcrypt password hashing, multi-factor authentication (coming soon) Secure account access
Access Control Role-based permissions, data isolation, audit logging Limit data access to authorized users only

5.2 Data Location and Storage

  • Primary Storage: AWS US data centers with enterprise security
  • Backup Systems: Encrypted, geographically distributed backups
  • Data Isolation: Complete user data separation using row-level security
  • Retention Controls: Automated data lifecycle management

5.3 Security Incident Response

In the event of a security incident:

  • We will investigate and contain the incident immediately
  • Affected users will be notified within 72 hours
  • We will provide clear information about what happened and steps taken
  • We will offer assistance and resources to affected users

Security Notice: While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. You can enhance your security by using strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication (when available), and keeping your account information confidential.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have comprehensive control over your data:

  • Data Sharing: Control what data can be shared with healthcare providers

6.3 Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • All personal health data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized usage statistics may be retained for service improvement
  • Backup copies are deleted within 90 days
  • You will receive confirmation of deletion completion
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance (billing records, etc.)

Free vs. Paid Account Rights: All users have the same privacy rights. However, paid users receive priority support for privacy-related requests and additional data control features.
